FAQs — Elara

(Confidence Coach for Sensitive, Ambitious Women)

Who is this coaching for?
This is for ambitious women who are sensitive, thoughtful, and often stuck in their own head — especially those who want confidence without losing their depth or empathy.
I overthink a lot. Will this actually help?
Yes. The work focuses on quieting mental noise, building self-trust, and helping you respond instead of spiraling.
Do I need to be confident already to start?
Not at all. Confidence is built through clarity and alignment — that’s what we work on together.
Is this mindset coaching or emotional healing?
It’s practical mindset and confidence coaching, grounded in self-awareness and real-life application — not therapy.
How long before I notice changes?
Most clients notice shifts in clarity, self-trust, and emotional calm within the first few weeks.
Is this 1:1 or group-based?
Primary work is 1:1, with optional guided sessions and resources depending on the program.
